Pre-Ceremony Shuttles: 5:05PM-5:45PM

Shuttles will circulate between the Doris Duke Center and the downtown pickup location before the ceremony. Parking spaces are also available at the Gardens.

Ceremony: 6PM

The ceremony will begin promptly at 6PM. All of those in attendance will act as our officiants. A coctail hour will follow as the wedding party scramble to take photos while the sun sets. Please remember to sign our wedding certificate with the provided pen before the end of the evening.

The reception will be indoor/outdoor. We will all eat under a big tent, and the dance floor will be inside. It's open bar! Please take advantage of the shuttles or ride sharing services. More details to follow in the program.
